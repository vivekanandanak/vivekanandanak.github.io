About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Department of Materials Science at National Tsing Hua University (NTHU), Taiwan. My expertise lies in AAO-templated nanowire fabrication, Zn/ZnO oxidation mechanisms, electrochemical sensing systems, and high-entropy alloy processing using die-casting and DLP additive manufacturing.

With a dual background in Aeronautical Engineering (B.E., M.Tech) and a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering (Nanomaterials), my research bridges advanced materials science with aerospace and industrial applications. I have published multiple high-impact articles in journals such as Chemosphere, Ultrasonics Sonochemistry, ACS Sustainable Chemistry & Engineering, and Journal of Alloys and Compounds.
